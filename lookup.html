Salary Calculation Formulas

Basic Salary: 50% of CTC (All Tiers)
HRA:
• Tier 1: 50% of Basic (25% of CTC)
• Tier 2: 40% of Basic (20% of CTC)
• Tier 3: 35% of Basic (17.5% of CTC)
CCA:
• Tier 1: 20% of Basic (10% of CTC)
• Tier 2: 10% of Basic (5% of CTC)
• Tier 3: 0% of Basic (0% of CTC)
ESI (Employee & Employer):
• If Gross Salary > ₹21,000: ESI = 0
• If Gross Salary ≤ ₹21,000:
  - Employer ESI: 3.25% of Gross
  - Employee ESI: 0.75% of Gross
Special Allowance: Remaining amount to reach target Gross Salary (always ≥ 0)
Net Salary: Gross Salary - Total Employee Deductions
